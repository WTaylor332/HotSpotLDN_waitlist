Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 20 June 2025


Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what personal data Hot Spot London ("we", "us") collects from you, through our interactions with you on this waiting list form, and how we use that data.

1. Data Controller

Hot Spot London is the data controller for the information you provide.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following information when you sign up:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if you choose to provide it)
  • Your consent choices for marketing communications

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To notify you via email when tickets for the Summer Day Party become available.
  • To send you important updates about the event.
  • If you opt-in, to send you marketing communications about future events that we think you might be interested in.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Our legal basis for collecting and using the personal information described above is your **consent**, which you provide by ticking the checkbox on our sign-up form (in accordance with GDPR Article 6(1)(a)).

5. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data until 30 days after the event has concluded. If you have consented to receive marketing communications, we will retain your contact details for that purpose until you unsubscribe.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • The right to **access** - You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
  • The right to **rectification** - You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.
  • The right to **erasure** - You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to **withdraw consent** - You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time.
  • The right to **lodge a complaint** with a supervisory authority (like the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) in the UK).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.
